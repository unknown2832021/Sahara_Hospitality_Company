Company Profile

Sahara Hospitality Co. SAOG (SHC) is a joint stock company listed on the Muscat Securities Market in the Sultanate Of Oman.
The funding objective of the company is to build, own and operate. The company is empowered to seeking additional opportunities within and outside of Oman.

Operational Highlights

The Directors and Management are proud to state that there were no Lost Time Injuries (LTI) during the last 19 years of operations in Fahud, 13 years in Nimr and 7 years in Rima. In recognition of the above, PDO awarded a commemorative plaque during the previous year.
