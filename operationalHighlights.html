Operational Highlights

Catering and Supplies Co. LLC (“CSC”), the service provider for PACs is responsible for the day to day facility management, provision of all required services and maintenance of SHC assets.


Catering and Supplies Co. LLC, retaining a service history of over three decades in Oman for providing catering & life support services mainly to Oil and Gas contractors in the interior of Oman and coastal area is a major founder shareholder of SHC. From beginning to date its performance has been found satisfactory in rendering all services free of complaints to multinational residents and visitors. The success, to a great extent, lies in SHC philosophy of strict adherence to Health, Safety and Hygiene policies.
