Operational Highlights

Catering and Supplies Co. LLC (“CSC”), the service provider for PACs is responsible for the day to day facility management, provision of all required services and maintenance of SHC assets.


Catering and Supplies Co. LLC, retaining a service history of over three decades in Oman for providing catering & life support services mainly to Oil and Gas contractors in the interior of Oman and coastal area is a major founder shareholder of SHC. From beginning to date its performance has been found satisfactory in rendering all services free of complaints to multinational residents and visitors. The success, to a great extent, lies in SHC philosophy of strict adherence to Health, Safety and Hygiene policies. In proof there of, please note the following statistics for over 9 years of operations:


The Directors and Management are proud to state that there were no Lost Time Injuries (LTI) during the last nine years of operations in Fahud and seven years in Nimr. In recognition of the above, PDO awarded a commemorative plaque during the previous year.
